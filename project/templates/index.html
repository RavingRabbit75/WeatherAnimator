About this app


Have you ever wanted a little advanced warning of rain or snow without having to constantly check a weather app or news?

Weather Animator is a fun little app that displays animations that reflect the current weather conditions of a specified city in the world. At the same time it allows a user to be notified of specific weather conditions they may be looking out for in advance.

It should go without saying that predicting the weather can be tricky. While this app does pull weather data from a reliable source, weather forecasts can still be wrong. Regardless of this, I hope this app at very least will prepare you every so often from getting rained on.

Raymond Chow
